Shipping & Return Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Shipping Estimates & Fees

We partner with leading shipping carriers (Delhivery, BlueDart, and Shiprocket) to ensure safe and prompt delivery of your ethnic wear:

  • Prepaid Orders: 100% Free Shipping on all orders prepaid via Credit/Debit card or UPI across India.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available at no additional fee.
  • Processing Time: Orders are dispatched from our Jabalpur workshop within 24-48 hours.
  • Delivery Timelines: Major metro areas receive deliveries within 3-4 days. Rest of India takes 5-7 business days. Jabalpur deliveries are fulfilled within 24 hours.

Returns & Exchanges Policy

If you are not completely satisfied with the fit or style of your purchase, we support a hassle-free 7-day return policy:

  • You can request returns or size exchanges within 7 days of package delivery.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, unaltered, and with all original tags attached.
  • Process: Initiate a return by emailing us at sales@missaraclothing.com or calling +91 9713962329.
  • Refunds: For prepaid orders, the refund is credited back to the original payment method. For COD orders, we issue a store coupon voucher or bank transfer within 5 working days of getting the returned item back.
