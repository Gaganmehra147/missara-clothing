Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

At Missara Clothing, we are highly committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, process, protect, and utilize your personal details when you browse and purchase from our e-commerce platform.

Information We Collect

When you visit or make a purchase from Missara Clothing, we collect certain personal metrics needed to fulfill orders and update services:

  • Contact details (name, email address, mobile phone number).
  • Delivery details (street address, city, state, postal code).
  • Session analytics cookies to understand site performance and manage cart states.

How We Use Your Details

Your details are primarily utilized to process shopping cart orders, issue invoices, confirm payments, provide delivery tracking notices, and respond to custom design messages sent via our contact page.

Data Security

We implement standard visual encryption layouts and browser safety mechanisms to ensure that your address records and orders data are kept highly secure. We never sell, rent, or distribute buyer information to third-party marketing companies.

Cookies Policy

Our website utilizes local storage cookies to retain cart items count, current wishlist tags, and session configurations. You can choose to clear or disable cookies in your browser settings, though doing so might affect cart state persistence.
